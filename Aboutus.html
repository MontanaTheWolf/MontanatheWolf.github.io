About us

Georgia Customs is a family owned autoshop located in the Augusta area. Cars are our passion and we want to share our passion with the people of Augusta, GA.

Georgia Customs specializes in domestic and foreign vehicles.

We are located at 1505 C North Leg Rd. 30909 with a 5 bay garage.
